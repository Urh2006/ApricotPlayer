## What's new?

- Added a `Cookies from browser` setting for YouTube sign-in and bot-check errors.
- When enabled, ApricotPlayer passes browser cookies to yt-dlp for search, playback, playlist/channel loading, and downloads.
- Supported choices are `none`, `chrome`, `edge`, `firefox`, `brave`, `chromium`, `opera`, and `vivaldi`.
- YouTube bot-check errors now explain that you should open Settings and choose the browser where you are signed in to YouTube.
