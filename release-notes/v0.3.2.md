## What's new in 0.3.2

- Settings are now split into a keyboard-friendly section list: General, Playback, Downloads, Cookies and network, and Updates and advanced.
- If a cookies file is set, ApricotPlayer now uses that file only and does not also try to read Chrome/Chromium browser cookies.
- Added `Export browser cookies to cookies.txt` in Cookies and network. This exports browser cookies to `%APPDATA%\ApricotPlayer\cookies.txt`, sets that as the cookies file, and switches `Cookies from browser` back to `none`.
- Download shortcuts now announce `Downloading audio...` or `Downloading video...` before the worker starts, giving screen readers time to speak the start message.
