## What's new?

- speed and pitch hotkeys now default to smaller `0.01x` changes
- settings now let you choose the speed step and pitch step used by the keyboard shortcuts
- settings now include a pitch control mode: `rubberband`, `mpv pitch`, or `linked speed`
- pitch control now defaults to the higher-quality rubberband audio filter
- `Ctrl+Shift+A` and `Ctrl+Shift+D` now work more reliably from both search results and player mode
- starting a download now announces `Downloading audio...` or `Downloading video...`
- settings now include `Show popup when download completes`; when disabled, completion is announced by the screen reader without a popup
