# What's new in version 0.8.52

- Previous playback now stays embedded, so `Ctrl+PageUp` no longer opens a separate mpv stream window.
- The low equalizer bands have been strengthened: 31 Hz and 62 Hz now use wider low-shelf filters, with 125 Hz also widened for clearer low-frequency response.
- Custom equalizer profiles can now be deleted from Settings or from the player equalizer dialog.
- Rapid volume-key repeats are coalesced before being sent to mpv, reducing repeated volume-change crackle on sensitive audio devices.
- Channel options now show only Videos, Playlists, and Popular, and Popular sorts channel videos by view count.
