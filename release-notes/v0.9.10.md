# What's new in version 0.9.10

- Fixed result-list letter navigation while playback controls are visible, so plain keys like P and V stay inside the results instead of triggering player actions.
- Kept Ctrl+PageUp and Ctrl+PageDown working from results for previous and next playback.
- Made next-after-dynamic-loading continue from the exact current video URL, preventing stale index or shuffle fallback from picking the wrong item.
- Limited player-triggered dynamic next loading to search and trending results, while folders and finite lists keep the normal no-next behavior.
