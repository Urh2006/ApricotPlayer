## What's new in 0.9.21?

- **Improved Lyrics Experience**: Synced lyrics are now displayed in a scrollable text box that prevents screen readers (like NVDA) from announcing the line numbers on every update. We also added options to **Copy** lyrics to clipboard and **Export** them to a .txt file!
- **Local Media Improvements**: When playing local folders or files, the player now intelligently hides YouTube-specific fields (like Views, Comments, Channel options) from the UI and metadata dialogs.
- **Bug Fixes**: 
  - Fixed a loud volume spike that could happen right at the start of a song.
  - Fixed an issue where checkboxes would behave erratically when toggled with the Spacebar.
  - Fixed the F5 key incorrectly announcing video duration.
- **Under the hood**: The codebase is undergoing a massive architectural split into smaller modules (pricot package) to make future development much faster and cleaner!
