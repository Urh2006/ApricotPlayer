# The Modularization & Stability Update

## What's New
- **Massive Codebase Split & Cleanup**: Completed the comprehensive modularization of the codebase, splitting `wx_main.py` into highly organized and cohesive mixin modules in the `apricot` package (under `apricot/ui/` and other directories). This drastically improves load times, maintainability, and code structure.
- **Settings Preservation**: Verified and secured user settings, history, and playlists. User data stored in `%APPDATA%/ApricotPlayer` remains completely untouched and perfectly preserved during this and all future updates.

## Fixes
- Restored the player to full working stability, fixing `NameError` exceptions, missing imports, and launch-time tracebacks introduced during refactoring.
- Cleaned up staticmethod contexts, resolving reference issues to `MainFrame` across modules.
