## What's new?

- updater install is now more direct and visible: after the update downloads, ApricotPlayer exits, replaces the `.exe`, restarts, and writes a log to `%APPDATA%\ApricotPlayer\updater.log`
- video details no longer appear in the tab order when a video opens; press `V` to show them
- when video details are open, `Escape` closes details and returns focus to the player; pressing `Escape` again returns to results
- `F2` toggles volume boost in the player, with `Volume boost on` and `Volume boost off` announcements
- when volume boost is turned off, volume is clamped back to 100 if it was above 100
- search results now support a batch download queue across searches
- `Shift+A` marks the focused result for audio download, and `Shift+D` marks it for video download
- selecting the same queued item again removes it from the batch queue
- the main menu now shows `Download all` when queued downloads are waiting
